Movie Recommendations A personal list — updated occasionally
⌄
The Lunchbox (2013) — Hindi • Drama
A mistaken lunchbox delivery leads to an unlikely correspondence between a lonely housewife and a widower in Mumbai. Quietly beautiful.
Piku (2015) — Hindi • Drama / Comedy
A warm, funny film about a working woman navigating life with her eccentric aging father. One of the best Hindi films of the decade.
Parasite (2019) — Korean • Thriller
A masterclass in storytelling about class and inequality. Bong Joon-ho's Palme d'Or and Oscar winner.
The White Tiger (2021) — English • Drama
A darkly funny and sharp look at class mobility and corruption in India. Based on Aravind Adiga's Booker Prize-winning novel.
