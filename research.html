Research

Barriers Within Borders: Port Access, Subsidy Asymmetry, and Fertilizer Nutrient Imbalance in India  (Working Paper) AAEA Annual Meeting 2026 · ISWA 2026
⌄
Documents how geographic isolation from import ports distorts fertilizer nutrient composition across Indian districts under asymmetric subsidy regimes.
Draft (PDF)
Agricultural Shocks, Farm Exit, and Farmer Suicides in India  (Work in Progress)
⌄
Examines how income volatility, land fragmentation, and exposure to agricultural shocks affect farmer welfare and farm exit decisions in India.
Measuring Flood-Induced Agricultural Production Losses Using Satellite Data: Evidence from Illinois  (Work in Progress)
⌄
Quantifies agricultural production losses from flood events in Illinois using satellite-based remote sensing data. Co-authored with Homa Taheri.
