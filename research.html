Earlier Research

“What Happened After LAF?”

Postgraduate Paper (2019–2020)

This paper studies monetary policy transmission in India in the post-liberalization period, focusing on sectoral effects of unanticipated tightening using structural VAR models. It examines changes in the relative importance of transmission channels before and after the introduction of the Liquidity Adjustment Facility (LAF).

Paper available here (PDF)

“Services Sector in the Indian Economy”

This thesis analyzes post-liberalization growth in India’s services sector, examining contributions to GDP, employment patterns, and structural shifts across subsectors.
